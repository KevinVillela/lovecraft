What is this?

This is an online version of Indie Boards and Cards's popular Don't Mess with Cthulhu board game. It was created by Dave Otaguro and Kevin Villela for the Plx team. Anyone is welcome to play it.

How do I play?

The gameplay is intended to be exactly the same at the rules defined here, so please read those first. After that, hit the Cthulhu icon in the top-left to get back to the main menu. Create a game, make some friends, give them the link, and start playing! Technically, 2-13 players are supported, but 6-10 is ideal. A couple of tips:
  • It is cheating to tell the investigator what position your cards are in (bug).
  • Click the cards to zoom in on them.
  • Mute the tab if the sounds are too annoying.
  • If something looks wrong, please refresh the page.
